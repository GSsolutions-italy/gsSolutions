# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Italian SMB owners, IT managers, and business decision-makers evaluating or managing Microsoft 365 for their organization. They want a reliable partner, not just a vendor — someone who handles the technical complexity so they can focus on running their business.

## Product Purpose
GsSolution is an Italian Microsoft 365 partner offering licenses, cloud migration, tenant management, domain configuration, and web development as bundled services. The core differentiator is a dedicated human agent per customer. Success means converting site visitors into qualified leads and booked consultations.

## Brand Personality
Reliable, human, expert. Professional without being cold. Italian B2B relationships — a trusted partner you call by name, not a ticket number.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages (navy + metric cards + cream background)
- Cold corporate Microsoft / MSP aesthetic (gray, enterprise-sterile)
- Busy agency portfolios or overly flashy Italian web design

## Design Principles
1. Partner, not vendor — language and visuals reinforce the human relationship at every touch
2. Earn trust through clarity — every claim is specific, no vague superlatives
3. One contact, zero confusion — the dedicated agent is the product; make it feel personal
4. Italian directness — copy is warm but concise, no filler
5. Microsoft credibility without Microsoft's aesthetic — certified partner positioning without borrowing the corporate blue/gray visual language

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Italian-language primary audience.
